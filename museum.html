The museum is organised in five departments :

2. ADMINISTRATION,FINANCE AND FACILITY MANAGEMENT DEPARTMENT- Policy and systems advisory ,accounting ,payroll,human resources /benefits managements, staff training,facility management,maintenance and security.

The lists of museums in Bihar is a list of museums,defined for this context as institutions that collect and care for objects of cultural,artistic ,scientific ,historical interest and make their collections or related exhibits available for public viewing .Museums that exists only in cyberspace (i.e,virtual museums )are not included.

SOME OF THE BEST MUSEUMS IN BIHAR


1. PATNA MUSEUM

Patna musuem is one of the oldest museum of India.It is locally known as JADUGHAR and situated in buddhamarg.It is in the style of mughal and rajput architecture .The main items displayed here are archaelogical objects,coins,arts paintings ,textiles etc.

children section displays wild life and artifacts and lastly the digital wall painting playing with background is extraordinary.

2. BIHAR MUSEUM

Bihar museum is a museum located in Patna . It was partially opened in August 2015.The chidren's museum ,the main entrance area ,and an orientation theatre were the only parts opened to the public in August 2015.Later ,in October 2017 remaining galleries were also opened.More than 100 arefacts were transferred here from Patna museum.

3. PATNA PLANETARIUM

Located in the city of Patna INDIRA GANDHI PLANETARIUM or PATNA PLANETARIUM is one of the oldest and largest planetariums of Asia. It is also popularly known as TARAMANDAL ,meaning a circle of stars , and is one of the largest and most well maintained planetariums in the country.A wide range of film shows on subjects related to astronomy are showcased here. Apart from that, exhibitions are also held on various related topics for visitors.

4. SRIKRISHNA SCIENCE CENTRE

Shrikrishna science centre is a science museum in Patna ,Bihar,India.which was named after the first chief minister of Bihar, SHRI KRISHNA SINHA .It was inaugurated on 14 April 1978,by the minister of education ,SHRI THAKUR PRASAD SINGH.Shrikrishna science centre forms a unit of the national council of science museums ,an autonomous body under the ministry of culture ,government of India.It is located in the southwestern corner of the Gandhi Maidan .The science centre has been set up for the benefit of the public ,especially students .Anew innovation hub also been set up to promote critical thinking through activities in science .Various facilities like 3D printing,broadband internet facilities as well as advanced labs are available .

5. JALAN MUSEUM

The Jalan Museum ,Quila house,is a private museum located in Patna ,Bihar ,India . It is one of the only two private museums in Bihar state .The museum has a personal collection of the great Jalan family.It is it self a history of patna ,the collection are rare ,Aditya Jalan grandson of late Hiralal Jalan take pride in telling the story how this museum grew and at one time his grandmother sold her expensive jewellery to sustain this museum and documents .The museum is not open for public ,but the family allows visit if prior appointment is taken ,and the visitor has to provide personal identity."DASTAN OF PATLIPUTRA"a documentry film on story of patliputra empire by Ramji Mishra Manohar has special references of this museum must see before visiting this museum.

7. NALANDA ARCHAELOGICAL MUSEUM

NALANDA ARCHAEOLOGICAL MUSEUM founded in the year 1971.Nalanda archaeological Museum has excavations from Rajgir and Nalanda ,an ancient university and a monastic complex .Thre are around 13463 antique items here,out of which 349 are displayed in the 4 main galleries .The archaelogical items that were found from Nalada can be traced back between 5th and 12th centuries AD.However,those brought from Rajgir are even older. Museum consists of sculptures and statues that are in stucco, stone,terracotta and through majority are in Basalt stone .Images and paintings found here belong to Buddhism,Jainism and Hinduism.
